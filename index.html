Keep an eye on your city

Understand multi-scale city dynamics

CrowdInsights is a digital solution that actively monitors urban environments detecting the composition, dynamics and motivations of crowds.
It provides data analytics and actionable insights to support decision-making and planning among administrations and businesses.

Key Features


COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES

CrowdInsights provides comprehensive & cross-sourced data, it integrates data collected from IoT sensors, mobile phones and social media to generate comprehensive, multi-scale views on urban dynamics. Its solutions includes both real-time & post-hoc results.
CrowdInsights offers data analytics and actionable insights to urban administrators, public security officers, commerce operators, shops managers, tourist offices and agencies, events managers.


MULTI-SCALE PERSPECTIVE

CrowdInsights provides perspectives of city dynamics at different levels based on several types of data.
CITY LEVEL: people demographics using Mobile Telco Data and Social Media. STREET LEVEL: counting of people & trajectories using commercial IoT sensors. POIs LEVEL: provides insights on how people pass, enter and stay, and what they do using IoT, Social Media and an App, offering personalized information, city loyalty cards, digital coupons.

Our Solution

CrowdInsights provides decision makers with reliable predictions and actionable insights on the composition, dynamics, and intentions of crowds in the urban environment. Understanding people’s behavior is crucial when, for example, a city manager has to decide opening times of a pedestrian area, or when commerce operators want to deliver effective marketing campaigns. CrowdInsights exploits many data sources, including Apps and Mobile Telco data, Social Media, and simple IoT sensors in the city areas and shops.


Industrial Partners


  • TIM (Telecom Italia) is the largest telecommunications company in Italy. The Semantics and Knowledge Innovation Lab (SKIL) focuses on Big Data and Data Mining, while the Smart Social Spaces Lab's main topics are technologies and services for “smart” interaction between people and environments.


  • Fluxedo is an Italian hi-tech startup on socialmedia analysis, realtime bigdata, and social planning. Fluxedo operates within the scientific park ComoNext and with the support of the incubator Polihub. Fluxedo will also act as "Business Champion" for the CrowdInsights project.


  • KPN is a Dutch landline and mobile telecommunications company. KPN makes available to the project its LoRa network and hundreds of LoRa-compatible sensors, which allow monitoring of location by GPS, motion, plus other environmental properties and storage of the observations in its cloud.

Research Partners


  • Fondazione Bruno Kessler (FBK) is a private non‐profit research center working for the public interest. The research group involved within CrowdInsights is i3 Intelligent Interfaces and Interaction, whose scientific focus is on technologies that facilitate human communication and interaction.


  • Politecnico di Milano is ranked as one of the most outstanding European universities in Engineering, Architecture and Industrial Design. Within CrowdInsights, the Data Science team (DEIB) will bring expertise in real-time and post-hoc Big Data Analytics and supporting the development of digital solutions.


  • TU Delft is regularly ranked in the top 15 technical universities in the world. TU Delft is the largest and oldest Dutch public technical university in the Netherlands. Within CrowdInsights, Tu Delft will act as a bridge to Amsterdam and bring crowd movement/tracking algorithms expertise.
